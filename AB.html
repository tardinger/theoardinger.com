Vignelli Accordion Book

❮ ❯

Most known for his work on the NYC Subway map, Massimo Vignelli is an Italian graphic and product designer and architect whom I find a lot of inspiration in. I researched, designed, printed and produced an accordion book of his most notable works and words. It is important to point out the use of the font Helvetica in this accordion book. I chose to do this because Vignelli was responsible for a lot of the branding/popularization regarding the typeface.
